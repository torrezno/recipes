Ingredients:
 - 1 tortilla
 - 2 tbsp hummus
 - 1 cup of lettuce mix (carrot, lettuce...)
 - 1 tsp olive paste
 - 1 tps nutritional yeast
 

Directions:
 1. Cut the lettuce mix in Jouliane
 
 2. Spread the hummus and the olive paste into the tortilla
 
 3. Put the lettuce in the tortilla

 4. Sprinkle the yeast 

 5. Roll the tortilla
